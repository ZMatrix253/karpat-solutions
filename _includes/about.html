About Me

Systems-Focused Simulation & Computational Engineer

I’m Zoltan Marton, a mechanical and biomedical engineer with a strong interest in systems modeling, simulation, and programming-driven engineering workflows.

My work sits at the intersection of physical systems, biological constraints, and software, where engineering problems are often poorly framed or overly manual. I use simulation and code not just to generate results, but to clarify assumptions, explore system behavior, and make engineering reasoning more repeatable.

My background includes simulation algorithm development for Carleton University’s flight simulator, biomedical and mechanical modeling projects, and building computational tools using Python, MATLAB, and ANSYS.

Karpat Solutions serves as an independent engineering platform where I develop, document, and share simulation-driven approaches to complex engineering problems, while exploring future collaboration and consulting opportunities.
