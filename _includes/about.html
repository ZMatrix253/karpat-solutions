About Me

Engineering Simulation & Computational Analysis

I’m Zoltan Marton, a Simulation & Computational Engineering Consultant specializing in CFD, FEA, CAD modeling, algorithm development, and high-precision engineering simulations. With experience in projects across mechanical, biomedical, and aerospace fields, I help engineering teams and businesses improve product performance, reduce development time, and make data-driven decisions using simulation.

My mission is to support engineering teams by providing reliable simulation expertise and delivering clear, actionable results.

Let’s work together to turn complex engineering challenges into optimized solutions.
