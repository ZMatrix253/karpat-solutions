About Me

Systems-Focused Simulation & Computational Engineer

I’m Zoltan Marton, a systems-focused mechanical and biomedical engineer with a strong emphasis on simulation, computational modeling, and programming-driven engineering workflows. My work spans mechanical, biomedical, and aerospace-related projects, where I use simulation to understand system behavior, test assumptions, and explore design trade-offs.

I build computational models and engineering tools using Python, MATLAB, and ANSYS, integrating numerical methods, automation, and system-level thinking to reduce manual effort and improve engineering clarity. Rather than treating simulation as a black box, I focus on model structure, validity, and reproducibility.

Karpat Solutions serves as an independent platform for developing and applying simulation-based approaches to complex engineering problems, with openness to future collaboration and consulting opportunities.
